Tonight
The run

How it works

1

Set up your performance

Create a performance for tonight. This gives you a private link for your audience.

2

Share it in the room

Place it where people can access it privately, or send the link directly. Give them space to respond on their own, without being put on the spot.

3

Let responses come in

Responses collect while the window is open. You can keep it open as long as you want.

4

Close and generate

When you are ready, generate the reading. All responses are combined into a single collective view.

5

Read what the room experienced

You do not read individual responses. You read the room.

No setup needed beyond this. You can run your first performance in a few minutes.
